Okapi Holiday Plans

The okapi, also known as the forest giraffe, loves to spend its holidays in the dense rainforests. Here's a sneak peek into an okapi's holiday itinerary:

  • Morning: A leisurely stroll through the misty rainforest, nibbling on fresh leaves and fruits.
  • Afternoon: A relaxing nap by a hidden stream, away from the hustle and bustle of the jungle.
  • Evening: A moonlit dance party with fellow okapis, complete with jungle beats and fireflies as disco lights.

Okapis sure know how to have a good time in the wild!
