Tapir Holiday Plans

The Schabrakentapir, or Malayan tapir, has some unique holiday plans. Here's what a typical tapir holiday looks like:

  • Morning: A refreshing swim in the river, followed by a hearty breakfast of leaves and twigs.
  • Afternoon: A luxurious mud bath to keep cool and fend off pesky insects.
  • Evening: Stargazing while munching on some delicious fruits, with the occasional splash in the water for fun.

Tapirs know how to relax and enjoy the simple pleasures of life!
